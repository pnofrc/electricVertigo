Welcome


Three parallel future time zones of 2041 are rising from the soil beneath the city of rotterdam.

Find the different sceneries which showcase one of these worlds to discover their hidden societies. Learn their secret language and find their hidden messages to make contact and complete the six main locations to be led to the final stage in which you will be trusted with a glimpse of the future. Play it all in one go or spread it out over a few days and return.
